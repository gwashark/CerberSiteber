Cerber was named after Cerberus, the gatekeeper of Hell, and lives in hell under the UK. She was entranced by the world of video games, anime, VTubers and idols filled with fun and luster, a stark contrast to the dark underworld she was all so familiar with.

Personality

She is a cheerful beacon of intense positivity and wholesomeness. As a hellhound, she nicknames everyone with "Wan" in their name and uses it like a verbal tick, such as switching "1" for wan at every opportunity. She is easily flustered by simple compliments, adding to her endearing nature.

Appearance

Standing at 4'1" (or, as she says it, 4 foot wan), Cerber has blonde hair styled in slightly wavy flowing twintails and violet-pink eyes. Her distinctive horns, which she insists are real (though fans debate whether they're dark chocolate or Styrofoam), are maintained by Hell magic to prevent melting in hell's heat. In her hellhound form, she transforms into an adorable 1 ft. tall (wan foot tall) puppy.
